# Cuoco

Cuoco is an interactive cooking-learning web app built as the group project for
the Processos de Gestão e Inovação (PGI) course at Universidade de Coimbra.
The brief was to take an idea through the full lifecycle of a startup, from
problem discovery to a working MVP and a final pitch. This repository holds the
final report that documents that journey, along with the course material that
supported it.

## The project

Around 70% of the University of Coimbra student community is displaced from
home, and many live alone for the first time without the time, budget, or
confidence to cook for themselves. Cuoco tackles the question of how to make
learning to cook more accessible and engaging for people who have little time
or experience. The name is the Italian word for "cook."

The core idea is "Duolingo for cooking": instead of a static collection of
tips and recipes, Cuoco teaches recipes through gamified, step-by-step
interactive lessons arranged in a learning tree that grows in difficulty as the
user progresses. The product is aimed at displaced students of the Department
of Informatics Engineering (DEI), with first-year students as the early
adopters.

### What the report covers

- **Value proposition.** The target market of displaced students, the problem
  of inaccessible and intimidating home cooking, and the interactive,
  gamified solution.
- **Lean Model Canvas.** The business model and how it evolved through user
  and mentor feedback, including a freemium model with a Premium subscription,
  ads, and commissions from local-business partnerships.
- **Product and MVP.** A React website with Google Analytics, including a home
  page with a call to action, a searchable and filterable recipe page, and the
  central interactive learning page (with a working drag-to-fry fried-egg
  demo). Store and social pages are in progress.
- **Promotion.** Branding and outreach through the @app.cuoco Instagram
  account, the website, and the in-class pitch (delivered in custom Cuoco
  t-shirts for extra impact).
- **Impact.** Validation through a student survey (73% wanted to improve their
  cooking, under 10% were uninterested in the proposed app) and interviews,
  plus results such as 221 Instagram followers and Google Analytics traffic
  data.

Delivered 20 December 2024.

## Repository structure

```
.
├── report/         Final Cuoco project report (the main deliverable)
└── lectures/       Course material that supported the project
    ├── intellectual-property.pdf   Guest lecture on IP and asset protection (IPN)
    ├── templates/                  Submission templates used to build the report
    └── exam-prep/                  Study material for the written exam
```

### report/
- `report.pdf` is the final submitted Cuoco report and the main deliverable.

### lectures/
- `intellectual-property.pdf` is the guest lecture on intellectual property and
  the protection of intellectual assets, given by Instituto Pedro Nunes (IPN).
- `templates/` holds the structures provided by the course:
  `final-report-template.docx` for the overall report and
  `proof-of-concept-template.docx` for the proof-of-concept and MVP section.
- `exam-prep/` holds `2018-normal-exam.pdf` (a past exam used for practice) and
  `summaries/`, a set of one-page topic summaries numbered by course topic.
