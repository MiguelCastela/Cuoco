# Innovation and Entrepreneurship Project

This repository holds the deliverables and supporting material for the PGI
group activity in the Innovation and Entrepreneurship course at Universidade
de Coimbra.

## About the project

The PGI activity asks each group to take an idea through the full innovation
cycle, from spotting an opportunity to validating it as a real business
concept. Rather than a purely theoretical assignment, it is structured like an
early-stage venture: the group identifies a problem or market opportunity,
defines a value proposition for a target audience, designs a business model
around it, and builds a proof of concept to test whether the idea holds up.

The final report documents that journey end to end. It covers the value
proposition and the type of innovation applied, the target market and early
adopters, the business model, and a proof-of-concept or MVP with the tools,
success metrics, and budget used to validate it. Intellectual property and the
protection of intellectual assets are woven in as well, reflecting the course
emphasis on turning ideas into defensible, market-ready ventures.

Alongside the project itself, the repository keeps the course material that
informed it: lecture content, the templates that shaped each section of the
report, and study material for the written exam covering management and
innovation, business models, startups, design thinking, IP protection, and
open innovation.

## Repository structure

```
.
├── report/         Final project report (the main deliverable)
└── lectures/       Course material that supported the project
    ├── intellectual-property.pdf   Guest lecture on IP and asset protection (IPN)
    ├── templates/                  Submission templates used to build the report
    └── exam-prep/                  Study material for the written exam
```

### report/
- `final-report.pdf` is the final submitted group report and the main
  deliverable of the project.

### lectures/
- `intellectual-property.pdf` is the guest lecture on intellectual property and
  the protection of intellectual assets, given by Instituto Pedro Nunes (IPN).
- `templates/` holds the structures provided by the course:
  `final-report-template.docx` for the overall report and
  `proof-of-concept-template.docx` for the proof-of-concept and MVP section.
- `exam-prep/` holds `2018-normal-exam.pdf` (a past exam used for practice) and
  `summaries/`, a set of one-page topic summaries numbered by course topic.
